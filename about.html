About MIS 4013

E-Commerce Web Design
MIS 4013

This course will cover conceptual topics and practical skills related to e-commerce and web design. Topics covered include technology and infrastructure for e-commerce, business concepts and social issues, and e-commerce in action. Students will use web technology tools to build e-commerce applications. Click the button "Course Information" to see the page with more information!
